Shipping Policy

Thank you for shopping with us! We strive to deliver your orders promptly and safely. Please read our shipping policy below.

📦 Order Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1–3 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed the next business day.

🚚 Shipping Methods & Delivery Time

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Availability
Standard Shipping 5–7 Business Days All Locations
Express Shipping 2–3 Business Days Major Cities
Overnight Shipping 1 Business Day Selected Locations Only

💰 Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and chosen shipping method when order is confirmed via whatsapp.

🚫 Shipping Restrictions

We currently ship within Pakistan. Unfortunately, we do not deliver to PO Boxes or APO/FPO addresses at this time.

📭 Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost or arrives damaged, please contact our support team within 7 days of the expected delivery date. We'll assist you in resolving the issue quickly.
